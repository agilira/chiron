# Welcome to Chiron

## Full-Width Landing Page

This page demonstrates the `hide_sidebar: true` feature. Perfect for:

- **Landing pages** with custom layouts
- **Simple sites** with just a few pages
- **Full-width content** like dashboards or charts
- **Standalone pages** like privacy policies

---

## How It Works

Add this to your frontmatter:

```yaml
---
title: Your Page Title
hide_sidebar: true
---
```

The sidebar will be completely hidden, and the content will use the full width available. The header navigation remains accessible!
